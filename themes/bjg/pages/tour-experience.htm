The Tour Experience

This one-on-one, one-day golf school gives you the true experience of being coached in a "Tour Environment".

The Tour Experience can be arranged on your time schedule (typical schedule listed below) and a Tour Experience Gift Bag is included featuring a Putting Alignment Mirror from Eyeline Golf, a pair of Nike Lunar 4 Golf Shoes, and one dozen Nike golf balls.

$1,050 per student

Morning Session

Complete equipment evaluation using Trackman and shot optimizer
Recommendations and electronic report generated and sent via email based on your current equipment
Full game evaluation and corrections (putting/wedge play/bunker play/full swing)
Recommendations, corrective notes and report sent via email
Full swing report generated through Trackman Combine
Ground force data report measured with Swing Catalyst

Afternoon Session

On course coaching/course management coaching
Course statistics recorded and report sent via email (fairways in regulation, greens in regulation, putts, and penalties)
Final Evaluation/Questions/Dismissal
Continuation programs available
